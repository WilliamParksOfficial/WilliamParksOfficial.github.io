Deep Blue Robotics

Deep Blue Robotics is the latest edition to Albemarle Robotics! We are FTC #18835, first established in 2021 due to the uproar of interest in our program here in Charlottesville, Virginia. At Deep Blue Robotics, we value Unity, Engagement, Commitment, Innovation, and Diversity. Our main goal is to make robotics accessible and fun for everyone regardless of their background. As a newly-established team, we have accomplished a lot and always set the bar higher each year!
