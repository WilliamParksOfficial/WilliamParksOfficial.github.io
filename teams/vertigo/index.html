VERTIGO

We are team Vertigo (7953), one of four teams at Albemarle High School, in Charlottesville, Virginia. We prioritize flexibility, teamwork, and effective, original designs with an emphasis on making our own choices and forging new paths. As the oldest of the four teams, we have been building and improving the communities around us since 2012, most recently helping to foster and mentor the new Journey Middle School lego league team.
