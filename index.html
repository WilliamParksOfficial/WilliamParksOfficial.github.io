Who We Are

We are a collection of 4 FTC teams located at Albemarle High School, Virginia, consisting of Firefly, Vertigo, Deep Blue, and Paradigm. You may visit the Teams page to learn about the teams individually, but we are one team in spirit.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to inspire children to be both capable of and interested in being leaders in the evergrowing fields of science and technology. We do so by engaging them in exciting programs that build science and technology skills, inspire innovation, and foster well-rounded life capabilities, including self-confidence, communication, and leadership.
