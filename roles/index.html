Roles

Our robotics teams have three diverse roles for you to choose from, each fun and exciting in their own way.

Building

Assemble the physical structure of the robot with various tools and materials. These can include, but are not limited to, CADing, 3D printing, and sets of metal channels.

Programming

Program the actions that the robot will take with the Java programming language in the Android Studio development environment.

Outreach

Reach out to potential sponsors for donations, as well as make connections in robotics.
